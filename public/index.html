More Information About Me


I'm a digital designer looking to kick off my career in the industry. After graduating from the University of Queensland with a Bachelor of Multimedia Design, I decided to relocate to the vibrant city of Sydney. I have a keen interest in user interface and experience design, and in my spare time can be found drawing or teaching myself front-end development.

My energy levels are high and my work ethic is strong. I'm eager to gain experience working with a motivated team, to apply my skill set and learn from those around me.

If you have a project you think I'd be well suited for, I'd love to hear about it - so don't hesitate to get in touch. You can also download my CV here.
