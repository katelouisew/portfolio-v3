Internship with Ironlak

Focus: Design and Communications



During my last semester of university I completed a three month internship with Ironlak. I spent one day a week at the Ironlak HQ, assisting the Art Department with any design work and social media content. The social media side of things saw me uploading and scheduling posts to their blogs and Instagram accounts. Although I have a personal Instagram and blog, this was my first introduction to handling social media for a well-known brand and I soon realised the amount of consideration and planning that goes into it.

In addition to social media, I completed various graphic design projects over the three month period. These included the creation of packaging templates, colour charts, POS kits and store artwork. By following the Ironlak style guide and working iteratively with the marketing manager, I completed all tasks to a high standard and in a time effective way.

Working alongside a team of creative and laidback graffiti-enthusiasts was a great experience. I learnt about the day-to-day operations of a brand that works hard to produce high quality products and output. I developed my skills within the Adobe Suite and my ability to use and benefit from constructive feedback. The small but passionate team inspired me to continue working towards whatever makes me happy, even when obstacles may get in the way.
