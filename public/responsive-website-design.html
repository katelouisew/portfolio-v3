Responsive Website Design

Client: Brisbane-based Producer, David Webster



For this project I designed a responsive website for Brisbane-based music producer, David Webster. While he already has Soundcloud and Bandcamp profiles for his work, he wanted to develop a stronger and more professional presence online.

David wanted a stripped-back and minimalistic style, similar to my personal design aesthetic. We agreed that a monochromatic colour scheme would work best with the embeded Soundcloud iFrames, allowing the inconsistent colours in the iFrames to sit cleanly on the white background.

I kept the layout simple but interesting, structuring content in a single column underneath the header, with the navigation as a sidebar on the left. My aim in having large amounts of white space around the content was to draw the viewers attention straight to the important information.
